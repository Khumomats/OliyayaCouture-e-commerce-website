ABOUT-US

OliyayaCouture was started by a black girl whose wildest dream was to become a fashion designer. Her passion for fashion

was discovered by her love for sketching which later on turned into recreating clothing and making them her own.

Her unconventional choice of style and craft has continued to grow vigorously over the years and caught the eyes of many and

inspired those around her.

Kenzo Takada once said that fashion is like eating, you shouldn’t stick to the same menu.

Her boundless and talent-filled craft is inclusive of traditional wear, bridal wear, farewell wear

and any other garments of the client’s choice.
